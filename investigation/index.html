Geotechnical investigation in Omaha starts with a clear understanding of the region’s layered loess hills and Missouri River floodplain deposits, which directly shape foundation risks and bearing capacity. A robust site characterization must comply with the Omaha Municipal Code and reference ASTM standards to address expansive clays and variable fill. Our CPT test provides continuous soil profiling and real-time pore pressure data, enabling precise stratigraphic correlation across these challenging glacial and alluvial sequences.

These investigations are essential for commercial developments in Aksarben Village, infrastructure along the Dodge Street corridor, and residential projects on the bluffs where slope stability is critical. The data gained from a cone penetration test feeds directly into foundation design and liquefaction assessments, reducing uncertainty for engineers and contractors. We deliver concise, actionable reports that keep your project on schedule and compliant with local building requirements.
